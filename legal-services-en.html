ANY BUSINESS IS AN AREA OF ACTIVITY THAT IS LIMITED BY STRICT LEGAL REQUIREMENTS

One of the main tasks of every business, regardless of its size and scope of activity, is to closely monitor the measures of legislative regulation, in order to prevent any difficulties in interaction with the regulatory authorities

  • Opening a legal entity that meets the requirements of the regulator for further obtainment of financial licenses;
  • Preparation of a set of documents for applying for licenses (Providing a Virtual Currency services, Operating as a Financial Institution, SPI, PI, EMI);
  • Assistance in drafting business plans;
  • Assistance in writing regulations and policies;
  • Assistance in recruitment (in accordance with the requirements of the regulator);
  • Assistance in software auditing for compliance with PSD2, PCI DSS and other EU and international legislation on the retention of personal data and data transfer;

  • Selection of ready to buy financial institutions;

    Conducting due diligence procedures;

  • Support of the purchase procedure / escrow agent services;

    Change of ownership/management + check for regulatory compliance;

  • Assistance in signing a card and processing white label agreement;
  • Obtaining Legal Opinion on doing business in EU, Turkey, CIS, Georgia;
